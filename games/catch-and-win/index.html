How to Play

  • Move your mouse (or finger on touch devices) left and right to control the catcher at the bottom of the screen.
  • Catch falling objects to score points. Different objects are worth different points.
  • Avoid missing objects. The game tracks how many you've missed.
  • Special objects:
    • Slow object: Decreases game speed
    • Fast object: Increases game speed
  • The game speed increases every 20 points you score.
  • Try to catch as many objects as you can and aim for a high score!
